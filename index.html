About Us

After sharing our new parenting experiences, we soon realised that
there was a huge opportunity to make the early stages of parenting a
lot easier and adaptive for the modern day parent. 2 years down the
track and a few sleepless nights later… Kiindred the app is here!

We hope it will give you the guidance and support you need, as you
embark on this exciting new parenting journey!

Tailored expert guidance, support & inspiration
for every stage of your new parenting journey
